Frequently asked questions

What is a credit?+
A credit is what you spend to book mentorship sessions. Credits leave your wallet only when you book, and unused credits stay there until they expire.
How many credits does a session cost?+
Each 1:1 session runs up to 30 minutes and costs 1–3 credits, depending on the mentor. The exact cost is shown on the mentor's profile before you book.
How long are credits valid?+

Validity is counted from the purchase date:

  • Focus (4 credits) — 2 months, no rollover
  • Growth (10 credits) — 4 months, plus 15-day rollover
  • Accelerator (25 credits) — 6 months, plus 30-day rollover
  • Custom credits — 60 days

Credits expiring soonest are used first. Buying another pack does not extend credits you already hold beyond their stated rollover.

Is this a subscription?+
No. Every pack is a one-time purchase — nothing renews and you're never charged automatically.
What happens if I cancel a session?+

It depends on when you cancel:

  • 12+ hours before — your credit returns to your wallet with its original validity
  • Within 12 hours — the credit is used, unless your mentor waives it
  • Mentor cancels or doesn't show — your credit returns with extended validity

For sessions booked less than 24 hours ahead, the cutoff is the halfway point between booking time and session start time.

How do I pay?+
Cards and UPI, processed securely by Razorpay. Prices are shown plus taxes; the exact total appears at checkout before you pay.
