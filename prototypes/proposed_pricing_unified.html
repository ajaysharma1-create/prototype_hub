Frequently asked questions

What is a credit?+
1 credit = one 30-minute 1:1 session with a mentor. Credits are deducted only when you book, and unused credits stay in your wallet.
Do all mentors cost the same?+
No. Each mentor requires 1, 2 or 3 credits per session, based on their experience. The requirement is shown on every mentor's profile.
How long are credits valid?+
The 4-credit pack is valid for 2 months with no rollover. The 10-credit pack is valid for 4 months plus a 15-day rollover, and the 25-credit pack is valid for 6 months plus a 30-day rollover. Custom credits are valid for 60 days. Credits expiring soonest are used first; buying another pack does not extend existing credits beyond the stated rollover.
Is this a subscription?+
No. Every pack is a one-time purchase. Nothing renews automatically.
What happens if I cancel a session?+
Cancel at least 24 hours before a session and your credits return to your wallet. Cancellations within 24 hours use the credits. If a mentor cancels or doesn't show up, your credits are returned along with 1 bonus credit.
How do I pay?+
Payments are processed securely by Razorpay. Cards and UPI are supported. Prices are shown plus taxes; the exact amount appears at checkout.
